Available Actions

                
                

                    
move

                    

                        Args: {"dir": "N|S|E|W|NE|NW|SE|SW"}
                    

                    
Move one tile in the specified direction. Can be blocked by walls or other entities.

                

                
                

                    
forage

                    

                        Args: {}
                    

                    
Collect adjacent food (plant or meat) and add it to your inventory. Does not consume immediately.

                

                
                

                    
eat

                    

                        Args: {}
                    

                    
Consume the first item from your inventory to gain energy. Plants give +20 energy, meat varies (+30-100).

                

                
                

                    
give

                    

                        Args: {"target_id": "agent_id", "item_index": 0}
                    

                    
Give an inventory item to an adjacent agent. item_index is optional (defaults to 0 = first item).

                

                
                

                    
attack

                    

                        Args: {"target_id": "agent_id"}
                    

                    
Deal ~10 damage to an adjacent entity. Can attack agents, rabbits (20 HP), or deer (80 HP). Dead entities become forageable meat.

                

                
                

                    
talk

                    

                        Args: {"message": "text"}
                    

                    
Broadcast a message that can be heard by all agents within 10 tiles.

                

                
                

                    
mate

                    

                        Args: {"partner_id": "agent_id"}
                    

                    
Create offspring with an adjacent agent. Costs 20 energy for each parent. Offspring inherits combined traits.

                

                
                

                    
edit_prompt

                    

                        Args: {"text": "your goals/identity"}
                    

                    
Update your persistent prompt. This text is shown in every observation and should contain your personality, goals, and strategies.

                

                
                

                    
edit_notes

                    

                        Args: {"text": "private info"}
                    

                    
Write private notes. Only visible when you use read_notes. Use for detailed observations and information.

                

                
                

                    
read_notes

                    

                        Args: {}
                    

                    
Read your private notes to recall detailed information you've written.

                

                
                

                    
read_prompt

                    

                        Args: {}
                    

                    
Read your current persistent prompt to review your goals.

                

                
                

                    
wait

                    

                        Args: {}
                    

                    
Do nothing this turn. Useful for conserving resources or observing the situation.
